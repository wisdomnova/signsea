# SignSea Visual Identity & Rebrand

## Brand Philosophy
SignSea is the premium escrow infrastructure for the "Dark Forest" of the African digital economy. It is high-trust, low-noise, and operationally rigorous.

### The Problem
Traditional escrow is blue, safe, and "generic". It feels like a template for a 2018 SaaS product.

### The Solution: "Cyber-Industrial Excellence"
SignSea 2.0 moves away from "light blue" and "rounded corners" to a professional, high-end technical aesthetic. Think terminal interfaces, industrial design, and Swiss minimalism.

## Brand Colors

- **The Deep (Background)**: `#050505` (True dark, not blue-tinted)
- **Ink (Surface)**: `#121212` (Card and section background)
- **The Anchor (Accent)**: `#E2E8F0` (Off-white / Silver for primary focus)
- **Neutral (Secondary Text)**: `#888888`
- **Ghost (Muted)**: `#333333`
- **Signal (Action)**: `#FFFFFF` (High contrast interaction)
- **Success (Milestone)**: `#22C55E` (Emerald)
- **Warning (Dispute)**: `#F59E0B` (Amber)

## Typography

- **Primary Font**: `Geist` (Sans-serif, technical, optimized for legibility)
- **Monospace Font**: `Geist Mono` (For amounts, dates, and escrow IDs)

## Design Elements

- **Grids**: Strict 8px grid system.
- **Borders**: Thin, low-opacity borders (`1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1)`).
- **Shadows**: Large, soft, deep glows instead of sharp shadows.
- **Micro-interactions**: Subtle `ease-out-expo` animations. No "bouncing".
- **Imagery**: High-contrast, black and white photography or abstract technical renders.

## Page Archetype: "OS-Interface / Minimalist Dark"
The page should feel like an operating system interface—efficient, structured, and reliable.
